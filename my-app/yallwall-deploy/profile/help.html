Frequently Asked

What is YallWall?

YallWall is a hyper-local, real-time chat platform for communities. Think of it as your city&apos;s 24/7 live-chat room where locals can share updates, ask questions, and connect with neighbors.

Who can see my posts?

Your posts are visible to everyone in your local area. We prioritize showing posts to people nearby, creating a true community feel.

How do likes work?

Double-tap any post to like it! Likes help surface the most engaging content to the top of the feed.

Can I delete my posts?

Yes! Tap the delete button (bottom right of your own posts) to remove them.
